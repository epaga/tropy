Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines the personal information that is collected from users and how it is used for the Traveling Tropy app.

Personal Information Collection

The personal information we collect from users includes their name, city, country, and email. This information is collected solely for the purpose of keeping track of contest entries.

Use of Personal Information

The personal information collected from users will only be used for the purpose of tracking Tropy contest entries. This information will never be shared, sold, or disclosed to a third party for any reason.

Protection of Personal Information

We take the protection of personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure it.

Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes to this policy will be communicated through our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us.
